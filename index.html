Featured Projects

Explore our recent projects

  • ShegaMart – E-commerce Platform Development

    We developed a full-featured e-commerce platform for ShegaMart, combining a sleek, responsive website with a custom mobile app for Android and iOS. The platform supports secure payments, real-time inventory, and user-friendly navigation, helping the client scale nationwide.

  • FitEthiopia – Health & Wellness Platform

    AppWorks designed and built an intuitive fitness tracking app tailored for the Ethiopian market. With personalized plans, live coaching features, and social integration, the app has significantly boosted user engagement and retention.

  • Habesha Bank – Online Banking System

    We partnered with Habesha Bank to modernize their digital services. Our team migrated their core systems to a secure cloud environment and redesigned their web interface for better usability and accessibility across devices.

  • AfroRide – Ride-Hailing App

    AppWorks designed and developed a scalable ride-hailing platform for AfroRide, including driver and passenger apps, real-time GPS tracking, and an intelligent fare calculation system. We also built a robust admin dashboard and backend infrastructure to support high traffic and secure transactions.
